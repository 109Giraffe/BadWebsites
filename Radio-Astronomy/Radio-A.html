What is radio astronomy?

Radio astronomy is a brach of astronomy where radio telescopes are used to study radio waves emitted by celestial objects. It alows us to observe phenomena that are invisible to the human eye and optical telescopes. By analyzing these radio signals we can see factors like the compositions and the structures of objects in space.
